I'm Pratham, and I blame Mr. Robot for all of this. Watching Elliot see systems the way most people never do flipped a switch in me: I wanted to understand how things really work underneath, and how they break. Years later that curiosity is my career. I recently earned my Master's in Information Systems from Syracuse University, though most of my real learning happens at 2am on TryHackMe (top 7% worldwide) or in the middle of a competition. My team won the CNY Hackathon in 2024, and I led Syracuse's team to a 2nd place finish at the Northeast Regional NCAE CyberGames.
What I love most is thinking like an attacker and a defender at the same time. I've worked across penetration testing, SOC operations, threat hunting, and red team engagements, and right now I'm fascinated by where AI and security collide, from breaking AI powered tools to understanding how attackers weaponize them.
When I'm not behind a keyboard, you'll usually find me at security conferences, swapping ideas, meeting people who are just as obsessed with this field, and keeping up with where it's all headed.
I believe every system tells a story. Some hide it, some reveal it. I want to find those stories and use them to build something stronger.
I'm open to full time roles, and I'll never say no to a security project, a CTF, or a good conversation at a conference. Let's connect.
